## Key Pairs and their naming convention

Certificate (Public Key)

- *.crt
- *.pem
- server.crt
- server.pem
- client.crt
- client.pem

Private Key

- *.key
- *-key.pem
- server.key
- server-key.pem
- client.key
- client-key.pem
